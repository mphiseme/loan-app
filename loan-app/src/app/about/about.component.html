All-Loans created by the company All In. All In started in late 2022. The company created by young developers who are focused on creating free tools for ordinary and professional people. These young developers met at a university call Bellevue University. They developed a friendship while working on a school project together. They decided to stay in touch after realizing that they had the same passions and desires. They believe that the world should have more products. A lot of them were able to accomplish school assignments by using free products created by other developers.

With the desire to give back to society, they came together to create the company called All In. They called it All In because of the strong conviction they have behind starting the company. The first free product that they created was called All-Art. All art is like the open-source photo editing software call Gimp. The second free application they created is called All Weather. It is a free weather app. It is their most popular application. 15,000 people download it from all application platforms. The All-Weather website has been visited by more than 30,000 people within the first 4 months of being released.

The loan calculator idea came into existence when one of the of the company’s member was paying a with credit card loan for years and couldn’t figure out the interest he paid so far. Figuring that they might be a common issue, they decided to create All-Loans calculator to help some out their stay on top of everything that have to do with their loans.
